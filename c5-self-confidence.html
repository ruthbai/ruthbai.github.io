What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Self awareness involves being honest to yourself, this helps to assess ourselves accurately and objectively see our greatest strengths and our biggest weaknesses. When we become honest to ourselves we get comfortable in our status. This doesn't necessarily mean that we let go of ourselves, because we know who we are clearly we can learn in better ways. Being mindful helps us to respect others this involves giving full moment to moment attention to other person while having a conversation. This would allow other person to feel more satisfied while having a conversation. Relationship between mindfulness and self awareness would be both mindfulness and self awareness involves people. being mindful please others, having self awareness please yourself and make yourself satisfied.
